About Us

Get to know more about us through our mission, vision and even a bit about our founders!

Founders

Mariam and Lili, founders of OMUN, are two extremely passionate individuals with wild dreams!! COVID-19 halted everything around them, except their powerful minds and never-ending creative processes. After extensive studies and meetings, they decided to establish a safe platform for youth from all around the world to debate global issues through. Their well-developed skills of planning, leading, communicating, and presenting aided them in recruiting, guiding and supervising OMUN 2020's board members as Secretary General and Deputy Secretary General.

Vision

At OMUN, we aspire to provide the opportunity of gaining an extraordinary MUN experience “from the comfort of your home”. A goal of ours is to develop all the skills required to ensure the excellence and proficiency of all delegates. Moreover, we desire to create an enabling, challenging, and inclusive environment where diversity is embraced. This offers participants from all around the world the chance to engage in fruitful debates despite the distance.

Mission

In order to fulfill such exemplary goals, our initiation plan is the key! Guidance will be provided by the most qualified team from the very beginning up to the very last moment of the conference! A detailed explanation of the topic will be provided through presentations, activities and many more. This ensures the deepest understanding of the topic in a fun, engaging style that enables all delegates to participate in a heated debate!!
